About Us

Eco Resilience Network (EReN) exists to champion environmental conservation and sustainable resource use while building climate-resilient communities through ecological restoration and biodiversity action.

By partnering with local changemakers to global organizations, we drive meaningful environmental progress. At our core, we empower youth and marginalized groups through education, innovation, and leadership tools — turning awareness into action for a future where people and nature thrive together.

Our Mission

  • Promote environmental conservation, protection, and sustainable use of natural resources.
  • Raise awareness and engage communities in climate resilience, ecological restoration, and biodiversity conservation.
  • Collaborate with local, national, and international partners to advance environmental goals.
  • Empower youth and marginalized communities through environmental education, innovation, and leadership development.

Our Vision

Empowering communities to build resilience, protect biodiversity, and lead eco-innovation.
